Common Face Shapes

Oval

Face length slightly longer than width, with balanced proportions and soft jawline.

Round

Width and length are similar, with a gentle curve along cheeks and jawline.

Square

Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline have similar width, creating a strong angular look.

Heart

Wider forehead tapering down to a narrow chin; cheekbones are prominent.

Diamond

Cheekbones are the widest part of the face; forehead and chin are narrower.

Oblong

Face length noticeably exceeds width, with straighter sides and longer proportions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a face shape?

Face shape refers to the structural outline defined by the proportions of your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and overall length.

Is this AI detector accurate?

Our AI engine achieves high accuracy when used under proper lighting with a clear, frontal face image.

Why should I know my face shape?

Understanding your face shape helps you choose the most flattering hairstyles, eyewear, and makeup styles.

This tool works best with a frontal image; side views may reduce accuracy.
